<h1 class="about-title">About</h1>

<div class="about-content">

<p>
Hi, I'm Amar — an LLM researcher working at the intersection of large language models, 
mathematical foundations of deep learning, and scalable AI systems. My work focuses on 
understanding how modern neural architectures learn, generalize, and reason.
</p>

<p>
This notebook is where I write about the ideas I encounter in my research and studies. 
Each post is an attempt to explain something clearly enough that my future self — and 
hopefully others — can learn from it. Topics range from the mathematical building blocks 
of neural networks (activation functions, optimization, linear algebra) to practical 
engineering challenges in training and deploying models.
</p>

<p>
I believe that the best way to understand something is to write about it. If you find 
an error or want to discuss a post, feel free to reach out.
</p>
